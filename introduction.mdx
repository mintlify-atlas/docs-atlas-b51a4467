## What is Trabajonautas?

Trabajonautas is a full-featured job portal built specifically for the Bolivian market. It supports:

- **Public job search** — anyone can browse and search available announcements without registering
- **Job seeker profiles** — registered professionals can track announcements and apply directly
- **Company management** — employers create company profiles and publish job announcements
- **Tiered plans** — free and paid subscription tiers with additional features for PRO users
- **Push notifications** — job seekers on PRO plans receive push notifications when new announcements matching their profile are published
- **Admin tools** — platform administrators manage users, areas, professions, and generate reports

## User roles

The platform has three distinct roles:

| Role | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **CLIENT** | Job seekers who browse and apply to announcements |
| **USER** | Employers who post announcements and manage companies |
| **ADMIN** | Platform administrators with full access |
