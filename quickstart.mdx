This guide walks you through creating an account and completing your first action on Trabajonautas — whether you are looking for work or posting a job.

## Choose your path

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Job Seeker">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Create your account">
        Go to the Trabajonautas homepage and click **Register**. You can sign up with your email address or use **Continue with Google** for a faster registration.

        After registering with email, check your inbox for a verification link and click it to activate your account.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Complete your profile">
        Once logged in, you will be prompted to complete your profile. Fill in:
        - **Full name** and **gender**
        - **Age**
        - **Phone number** (Bolivian format, country code +591 is added automatically)
        - **Location** — select your city or department in Bolivia
        - **Profession** — choose your field from the available list
        - **Education level** — select your grade profile

        A complete profile increases your visibility to employers.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Browse and search jobs">
        From the homepage, you can see recent job announcements. Use the search bar at `/busqueda` to search by job title. Filter results by area, profession, or location to narrow your results.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Apply to an announcement">
        Open any announcement to see the full details including title, description, salary, expiration date, and attached files. Click **Apply** to register your interest. You can track all announcements you have applied to from your dashboard at `/panel`.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Employer">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Create your account">
        Register on Trabajonautas using your business email or Google account. After email verification, your account will have the **USER** role, giving you access to employer features.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Set up your company">
        Navigate to **Admin → Companies** and click **New Company**. Enter your company name, type, and other details. You can manage multiple companies from a single account.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Post your first announcement">
        Go to **Admin → Announcements** and click **New Announcement**. Fill in:
        - Job title and description
        - Salary
        - Area and required professions
        - Location(s)
        - Expiration date
        - Attach any relevant files (requirements, job description PDFs, etc.)

        Toggle **PRO** on if you want to send push notifications to matching job seekers (requires a paid subscription).
      </Step>
      <Step title="Manage candidates">
        From the announcements list, view who has applied to each posting. Use the client management section to review applicant profiles and update your pipeline.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
