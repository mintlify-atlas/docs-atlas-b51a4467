Trabajonautas offers different account tiers to match how you use the platform. Every user starts with access to the free plan, and you can upgrade to a paid plan at any time from the **Plans** page (`/pro`).

## How account plans work

Your account is tied to an **account type** that determines which features you can access. When you subscribe to a paid plan, your account gains a `limit_time` — an expiry timestamp calculated from the plan's `duration_days`. Once that date passes, your account reverts to free-tier access until you renew.

<Note>
  The **Plans** page always shows the most up-to-date list of available plans, including each plan's name, price, and duration in days.
</Note>

## Available plan tiers

Trabajonautas offers plans for both **job seekers** (Trabajonautas) and **employers/recruiters**. The free plan is available to everyone with no payment required. Paid plans — such as **PRO** and **PRO MAX** — unlock expanded limits and visibility features for the duration of the subscription period.

## Plan comparison

| Feature | Free | PRO | PRO MAX |
|---|---|---|---|
| Browse job listings | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Apply to openings | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Access personal dashboard | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| View PRO-flagged announcements | No | Yes | Yes |
| Receive push notifications for new jobs | No | No | Yes |
| Timed subscription access | No | Yes (fixed days) | Yes (fixed days) |

<Tip>
  Visit the **Plans** page to see the exact price (in bolivianos) and duration in days for each paid plan before purchasing.
</Tip>

## Plan duration and expiry

Each paid plan has a `duration_days` value that defines how many days your upgraded access lasts. When your payment is verified, the system sets your account's `limit_time` to the current date plus that number of days.

For example, if a PRO plan has a `duration_days` of 30, your access expires 30 days after your payment is confirmed by the Trabajonautas team.

Once your `limit_time` is reached:

- Your account type resets to the free tier automatically.
- Your profile and history remain intact — you do not lose any data.
- You can renew or upgrade at any time by returning to the **Plans** page.

<Warning>
  Paid features become unavailable as soon as your `limit_time` expires. Renew before the expiry date to maintain uninterrupted access.
</Warning>

## Push notifications and your account

Push notifications for new job announcements are only delivered to **PRO MAX** (`account_type_id = 3`) subscribers. Your account can store a `device_token` that enables push notifications on your mobile device. This token is linked directly to your account and is saved automatically when you use the platform on a supported mobile device. No manual setup is required on your part.

<Note>
  PRO subscribers can view PRO-flagged announcements but do not receive push notifications — that feature is exclusive to PRO MAX.
</Note>

## Viewing and upgrading your plan

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the plans page">
    Navigate to the **Plans** page to see all available account types with their name, price, and duration.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a plan">
    Choose the plan that fits your needs and click the purchase button to proceed to the checkout page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the purchase flow">
    Confirm your location, profession, and payment details. Your subscription request is submitted to the Trabajonautas team for verification.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for payment verification">
    The Trabajonautas team manually reviews and confirms your payment. Once verified, your `limit_time` is activated and your account upgrades immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>
