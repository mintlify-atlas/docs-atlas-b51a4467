---
title: "Setting up your company"
description: "How employers create and manage company profiles on Trabajonautas"
---

Every job announcement on Trabajonautas is published under a company profile. Before you can post any convocatorias, you need at least one company set up and linked to your account.

## Navigating to your companies

Go to **`/admin/empresa`** from the dashboard to see all companies associated with your account. The list is sorted alphabetically by company name and is paginated with 8 results per page. Use the search bar to filter by `company_name`.

<Note>
  Companies are linked directly to your user account. Each company you create is owned by you and only your account can publish announcements under it.
</Note>

## Creating a new company

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the new company form">
    Navigate to **`/admin/nueva-empresa`** or click the **Nueva Empresa** button from the company list. This opens the company creation form.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Fill in the company details">
    Complete the following required fields:

    | Field | Description |
    |---|---|
    | `company_name` | The official name of your company. Must be unique across the platform — no two companies can share the same name. Minimum 2 characters. |
    | `description` | A text description of your company, its mission, or what it does. Shown to candidates on announcement pages. |
    | `company_image` | Your company logo. Accepted formats: JPG, JPEG, PNG, WEBP. Maximum file size: 4 MB. |
    | `company_type_id` | The type of company (e.g., private, public, NGO). Select from the available categories in the dropdown. |

    <Tip>
      Use a high-quality square logo for `company_image`. It appears as a thumbnail next to every announcement your company publishes, so a clear image makes your listings stand out to candidates.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save the company">
    Click **Guardar** to create the company. You are redirected back to `/admin/empresa` after saving. Your new company is immediately available to select when creating announcements.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Editing an existing company

To update a company's details, navigate to **`/admin/nueva-empresa/{company}`**, where `{company}` is the company's ID. You can also click the edit button directly from the company list at `/admin/empresa`.

All fields are editable including the logo. If you leave the logo field unchanged when editing, the existing image is kept. The `company_name` uniqueness rule still applies on edits — you cannot rename a company to a name already in use by another company.

<Note>
  Saving an edited company clears the cached company list used by the announcement form. Any changes to your company name will appear immediately when creating new announcements.
</Note>

## Managing multiple companies

You can create and manage as many companies as your account needs. All companies owned by your account appear in the `/admin/empresa` list regardless of their current status. This makes it straightforward to manage a portfolio of brands or client companies from a single account.

<Tip>
  Use the search field on the company list to quickly find a specific company by name when managing a large number of profiles.
</Tip>

## Soft-delete and restore

Deleting a company from the list performs a **soft delete** — the company record is not permanently removed from the database. Soft-deleted companies remain visible in the `/admin/empresa` list with a visual indicator so you can distinguish them from active companies.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Delete a company" icon="trash">
    Removes the company from active use. Soft-deleted companies cannot be selected when creating new announcements.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Restore a company" icon="rotate-left">
    Restores a previously deleted company back to active status. Use the restore button next to the company in the list.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Warning>
  Soft-deleting a company does not delete its announcements. Existing announcements linked to the company remain in the system. However, a deleted company will no longer appear in the company selector when creating new convocatorias.
</Warning>

## Company types

When creating a company you must select a **company type** (`company_type_id`). Company types are predefined categories managed by administrators (e.g., Empresa Privada, Institución Pública, ONG). If you do not see an appropriate type for your organization, contact the platform administrator.
